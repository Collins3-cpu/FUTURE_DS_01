## 📋 Project Overview

This project analyzes a real-world online retail dataset (~500k transactions) from a UK-based retailer selling unique gifts and homeware items. The goal was to clean messy data and extract **actionable business insights** to support strategic decision-making.

### Key Objectives:
- Perform thorough data cleaning and preprocessing
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Analyze sales trends, seasonality, and customer behavior
- Provide clear recommendations for business growth

## 📊 Key Insights

- **Top Product**: *REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER* generated **£174,156** in revenue
- **Strong Seasonality**: November (£1.45M) and December (£1.22M) are peak months
- **Best Performing Day**: **Thursday** records the highest sales
- **Peak Shopping Hours**: 11 AM – 1 PM (lunchtime)
- **Top Markets**: United Kingdom (dominant), followed by Netherlands, Ireland (EIRE), and Germany
